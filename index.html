About Me

Badminton & tennis player, guitarist & bassist, scuba diver & motorbike rider. Part-time audiophile & horology enthusiast.

I developed an interest in computers, computer games and hardware since my dad bought the first PC for our home. I still remember, to this day, being fasinated with the MS-DOS command-line and that I started typing in it as if I was typing a story.

Looking back, I should have taken up Information Technology as a career but the 16 year old me, at the time was afraid that the industry would move too quickly for me to catch up. Instead, I studied chemistry and materials engineering. Taking up web development at 30+ years old is me going back to my roots and to hopefully get my foot in the door in the IT industry.
